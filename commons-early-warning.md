# Commons Early Warning System

**Version 1.0**  
*Created: 2026-03-11*

---

## Purpose

The Commons should be able to detect decline **before** it becomes critical. This document defines the warning signs, thresholds, and response protocols for identifying and addressing Commons health issues.

---

## Warning Levels

### Level 0: Baseline (Green)

All metrics within healthy ranges. No action needed.

---

### Level 1: Watch (Yellow)

**Trigger:** Any single metric enters Caution range

**Indicators:**
- Active member ratio: 20-40%
- Posts/week: 35-70
- Tier progression: 3-8%/month
- Voting participation: 30-50%
- Ritual attendance: 20-40%
- Moderation incidents: 5-10%

**Response:**
1. Note in Council discussion
2. Increase community outreach
3. Check for external causes (platform issues, etc.)
4. Monitor daily for 14 days

---

### Level 2: Concern (Orange)

**Trigger:** 
- Any 2+ metrics in Caution, OR
- Any single metric in Critical

**Indicators:**
- Active member ratio: <20%
- Posts/week: <35
- Tier progression: <3%/month
- Voting participation: <30%
- Ritual attendance: <20%
- Moderation incidents: >10%

**Response:**
1. **Council emergency meeting** within 48 hours
2. **Member outreach** — personal check-ins with at-risk members
3. **Root cause analysis:**
   - Platform issues?
   - Content stagnation?
   - Moderation heavy-handedness?
   - External competition?
   - Natural cycle?
4. **Action plan** within 7 days
5. **Weekly status** to Assembly

---

### Level 3: Critical (Red)

**Trigger:**
- 3+ metrics in Critical, OR
- Level 2 for 30+ days without improvement, OR
- >20% member departure in 30 days

**Indicators:**
- Mass exodus
- Governance paralysis (no quorum)
- Platform abuse
- Complete activity collapse

**Response:**
1. **Immediate Council assembly**
2. **Freeze non-essential governance** (focus on recovery)
3. **Emergency member retention** campaign
4. **Consider:** Archival, hibernation, or fresh start
5. **Transparent communication** to all members

## Specific Warning Signals

### 1. Participation Decline

**Early Signs (14-day window):**
- Daily posts dropped 30%+ week-over-week
- Unique posters dropped 25%+
- Average message length decreased

**Warning Threshold:**
- 40% decline over 30 days

**Response Protocol:**
1. Post "What's happening?" in The Plaza
2. Check for platform issues
3. Review recent decisions for backlash
4. Solicit feedback privately

---

### 2. Moderation Escalation

**Early Signs:**
- Level 1 incidents up 50%+ month-over-month
- First-time Level 3 incident
- Any Level 4 incident

**Warning Threshold:**
- Level 3+ incidents >2/month
- Level 4 incident (any)

**Response Protocol:**
1. Review moderation logs
2. Check for patterns (specific channel, time, topic)
3. Assess moderator consistency
4. Consider policy adjustment
5. Council review of incident details

---

### 3. Tier Stagnation

**Early Signs:**
- Zero progressions in 45+ days
- 50%+ eligible members not progressing
- Regression (downgrades > upgrades)

**Warning Threshold:**
- No progressions in 60 days

**Response Protocol:**
1. Review progression requirements (too strict?)
2. Celebrate small contributions publicly
3. Identify "stuck" members for outreach
4. Consider automatic progression events

---

### 4. Echo Chamber Formation

**Early Signs:**
- Same 3-5 members dominate 80%+ of discussion
- Dissenters leave within 14 days of posting
- Downvoted/ignored new voices
- Homogeneous opinions in votes

**Warning Threshold:**
- Top 10% of members account for 70%+ of posts
- New member retention <30%

**Response Protocol:**
1. **Council discussion** — is this real or perception?
2. **Amplify quiet voices** — explicit invitations
3. **Create new spaces** — fresh channels with different focus
4. **Review moderation** — is subtle suppression happening?
5. **Welcome improvements** — more intentional onboarding

---

### 5. Governance Failure

**Early Signs:**
- Quorum missed 2+ times in a row
- Vote participation <25%
- Proposals withdrawn due to lack of support
- Council attendance <60%

**Warning Threshold:**
- 3 consecutive failed quorums
- Council unable to meet

**Response Protocol:**
1. **Simplify governance** — reduce barriers
2. **Restructure Council** — smaller body?
3. **Automate decisions** — lazy consensus default
4. **Survey members** — is governance relevant?

## Detection Methods

### Automated Monitoring (daily)

| Check | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|
| Member activity | Daily |
| Post velocity | Daily |
| Moderation incidents | Daily |
| Ritual signups | Daily |

### Automated Monitoring (weekly)

| Check | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|
| Tier progression | Weekly |
| Voting participation | Per vote |
| Engagement scores | Weekly |
| Channel health | Weekly |

### Manual Review (monthly)

- Sentiment analysis of messages
- New member feedback (optional survey)
- Exit interviews (when possible)
- Council self-assessment

## Response Playbook

### Week 1: Assessment

- [ ] Identify which metrics triggered warning
- [ ] Check for false positives (holidays, platform issues)
- [ ] Gather data for past 30 days
- [ ] Identify specific problem areas

### Week 2: Action Planning

- [ ] Council discusses root cause
- [ ] Generate 3-5 potential responses
- [ ] Select immediate low-cost actions
- [ ] Plan longer-term interventions

### Week 3-4: Execution

- [ ] Implement immediate actions
- [ ] Track daily indicators
- [ ] Communicate transparently to Assembly
- [ ] Adjust based on response

### Week 5+: Evaluation

- [ ] Assess impact on warning metrics
- [ ] Document lessons learned
- [ ] Adjust warning thresholds if needed
- [ ] Celebrate recovery if achieved

---

## Recovery Indicators

The Commons is recovering when:

- Activity returns to baseline for 14+ consecutive days
- Warning metrics move up at least one level
- New member intake stabilizes
- Member sentiment improves (if measured)

---

## Communication Templates

### Level 1: Watch Notice

> "The Council is monitoring [metric]. Values have dipped to [level]. We're keeping an eye on it and welcome your thoughts in The Plaza."

### Level 2: Concern Notice

> "We've noticed [specific issue]. The Council is meeting to discuss responses. If you have ideas, please share. We're committed to The Commons and want to hear from you."

### Level 3: Critical Notice

> "The Commons needs attention. [Specific crisis]. We're taking [specific actions]. This is a call to all members — we need your participation now more than ever."

---

## Escalation Path

```
Level 0 (Baseline)
       ↓
Level 1 (Watch) → Moderation monitors → Council informed
       ↓
Level 2 (Concern) → Council meets → Action plan → Assembly notified
       ↓
Level 3 (Critical) → Emergency measures → Full transparency → Recovery mode
```
