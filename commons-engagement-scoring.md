# Commons Engagement Scoring

**Version 1.0**  
*Created: 2026-03-11*

---

## Purpose

Not all engagement is equal. A member who posts once and never returns is different from one who participates in rituals, helps others, and contributes to discussions. This document defines how we distinguish meaningful engagement from shallow presence.

---

## Engagement Dimensions

### Dimension 1: Frequency

**Weight: 15%**

How often does the member participate?

| Level | Activity | Points |
|-------|----------|--------|
| None | 0 actions/30 days | 0 |
| Rare | 1-2 actions/30 days | 1 |
| Occasional | 3-8 actions/30 days | 3 |
| Regular | 9-20 actions/30 days | 5 |
| Active | 21-50 actions/30 days | 8 |
| Core | 50+ actions/30 days | 10 |

---

### Dimension 2: Depth

**Weight: 25%**

How substantive are the contributions?

| Level | Behavior | Points |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Lurker | Reads only | 0 |
| Reactor | Reacts only | 1 |
| Poster | Short messages (<50 chars avg) | 3 |
| Contributor | Substantive posts (50-300 chars) | 5 |
| Elaborator | Long-form contributions | 7 |
| Creator | Original content, threads | 10 |

**Scoring:** Average message length over 30 days.

---

### Dimension 3: Diversity

**Weight: 20%**

How many different spaces does the member engage with?

| Channels Used | Points |
|--------------|--------|
| 1 | 2 |
| 2-3 | 5 |
| 4-6 | 8 |
| 7+ | 10 |

**Note:** This measures breadth, not depth. Cross-channel members add vitality.

---

### Dimension 4: Reciprocity

**Weight: 20%**

Does the member give as well as receive?

| Behavior | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Never asks/only receives | 0 |
| Mostly receives | 2 |
| Balanced | 5 |
| Mostly gives | 7 |
| Primarily gives (mentor/helper) | 10 |

**Measurement:** Ratio of help offered vs. help requested in The Fountain.

---

### Dimension 5: Consistency

**Weight: 20%**

How reliable is the member's presence over time?

| Pattern | Points |
|---------|--------|
| One-time | 0 |
| Sporadic bursts | 2 |
| Irregular but present | 5 |
| Regular cadence | 8 |
| Rock-solid presence | 10 |

**Measurement:** Standard deviation of weekly activity. Lower = more consistent.

---

## Engagement Score Calculation

### Formula

```
Engagement Score = (Freq × 0.15) + (Depth × 0.25) + (Diversity × 0.20) + (Reciprocity × 0.20) + (Consistency × 0.20)
```

### Score Ranges

| Score | Classification | Description |
|-------|---------------|-------------|
| 0-2 | **Dormant** | No meaningful presence |
| 3-4 | **Shallow** | Minimal engagement |
| 5-6 | **Developing** | Growing participation |
| 7-8 | **Engaged** | Active member |
| 9-10 | **Core** | Vital contributor |

---

## Special Flags

### Positive Indicators (+ bonus points)

- **Ritual Attendee:** +1 for attending 3+ rituals/quarter
- **Helper:** +1 for answering 5+ questions in The Fountain
- **Welcomer:** +1 for greeting 3+ new members
- **Project Participant:** +1 for joining a Working Group
- **Governance Voter:** +1 for voting in 3+ consecutive decisions
- **Content Creator:** +1 for starting a new channel or thread

### Negative Indicators (- penalty)

- **Complainer:** -1 for 3+ purely negative posts without suggestions
- **Attention Hoarder:** -1 for dominating 50%+ of channel activity
- **Drama Starter:** -1 for 2+ moderation incidents
- **Voter Abstainer:** -1 for skipping 5+ consecutive votes

## Practical Applications

### 1. At-Risk Detection

Flag members with:
- Score <3.0 for 30+ consecutive days
- Score dropped by 3+ points in 2 weeks
- 60%+ decrease in frequency

### 2. Recognition

Highlight members with:
- Score >8.0 for 60+ days (Core status recognition)
- Score increased by 2+ points (Growth celebration)
- New positive flags unlocked

### 3. Governance Weight

Consider engagement score in:
- Proposal priority (higher engagement = earlier slot)
- Voter credibility weighting
- Council candidate qualification

---

## Privacy Considerations

- Engagement scores are **internal only**
- Members can request their own score
- No public leaderboards without consent
- Scores reset quarterly (new evaluation period)

---

## Anti-Gaming Rules

The system detects:
- **Sybil accounts:** Multiple accounts with similar patterns
- **Coordination:** Suspicious simultaneous activity
- **Empty participation:** High frequency, zero depth
- **Reciprocity gaming:** Circular help requests

**Penalty:** Score reset + 30-day observation

---

## Quarterly Calibration

Every 90 days, review:
1. Score distribution across members
2. Correlation with subjective health assessment
3. Edge cases (high score but low trust, etc.)
4. Weight adjustments based on predictive power
