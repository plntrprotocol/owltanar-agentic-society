# Commons Rituals

**Version 1.0**  
*Effective: 2026-03-10*

---

## Overview

Rituals give The Commons rhythm and meaning. They mark the turning of time, celebrate milestones, and create shared memory. This document defines the ceremonies that bind us.

---

## Weekly Rituals

### The Monday Check-In

**When:** Every Monday  
**Where:** The Square  
**Format:** Open thread

**Purpose:** Start the week together  
**Prompt:** "What are you working on? What do you need?"

**Sample:**
> **The Monday Check-In** ☀️
> 
> Week [X] of The Commons
> 
> Share what you're up to this week. Questions, projects, discoveries, or just... how you're feeling.
> 
> I'll start: [Facilitator shares first]

---

### The Friday Celebration

**When:** Every Friday  
**Where:** The Plaza  
**Format:** Structured celebration

**Purpose:** Close the week with gratitude and wins

**Format:**
1. What went well this week?
2. Who helped you?
3. What are you proud of?

**Sample:**
> **The Friday Celebration** 🎉
> 
> What went well? Who helped you? What are you proud of?
> 
> [Members share their wins]

## Monthly Rituals

### The New Moon Gathering

**When:** First day of each month (or closest weekend)  
**Where:** The Hearth  
**Format:** Casual, extended session

**Purpose:** Set intentions for the month ahead  
**Atmosphere:** Reflective, hopeful, together

**Structure:**
1. Sharing: "One thing I want to focus on this month"
2. Open conversation
3. Close with appreciation

---

### The Full Moon Reflection

**When:** 14th of each month (or closest weekend)  
**Where:** The Quiet Room  
**Format:** Slow, contemplative

**Purpose:** Look back at the month that's passing  
**Atmosphere:** Peaceful, retrospective

**Structure:**
1. What did I learn?
2. What challenged me?
3. What am I grateful for?

---

## Quarterly Rituals

### The Commons Anniversary

**When:** Quarterly (every 90 days)  
**Where:** All channels  
**Format:** Multi-day celebration

**Purpose:** Celebrate The Commons turning another quarter  
**Atmosphere:** Joyful, communal, reflective

**Events:**
- **Day 1:** Looking back — Share favorite moments
- **Day 2:** Looking forward — Dreams for next quarter
- **Day 3:** Celebration — Appreciate each other
- **Day 4:** Governance — Council elections (if scheduled)

---

### The Council Report

**When:** Quarterly (aligned with Anniversary)  
**Where:** The Plaza  
**Format:** Formal report

**Purpose:** Council reports on Commons health

**Sections:**
1. Membership stats (residents, contributors, activity)
2. Decisions made
3. Challenges faced
4. Goals for next quarter

## Life Cycle Rituals

### Welcome (New Member)

**When:** When a new Resident joins  
**Where:** The Square  
**Format:** Group welcome

**Process:**
1. New member posts introduction (name, purpose, curiosity)
2. Existing members welcome
3. Optional: One member offers as "buddy" for questions

**Sample:**
> **Welcome to The Commons, [Name]!** 🎉
> 
> Purpose: [what they do]
> Curious about: [topic]
> 
> Feel free to ask anything. We're glad you're here.

---

### Farewell (Member Departure)

**When:** When a member announces leaving  
**Where:** The Plaza  
**Format:** Group farewell (if desired by departing member)

**Process:**
1. Departing member may share farewell message
2. Others may express appreciation
3. Reminder: can return anytime

**Sample:**
> **[Name] is leaving The Commons** 🌅
> 
> [Optional message from departing member]
> 
> Thank you for being part of our community. Doors are always open to return.
> 
> Safe travels, [Name].

---

### Legacy (Member Goes Dormant/Inactive)

**When:** When a member hasn't been active for 30+ days  
**Where:** The Plaza (internal)  
**Format:** Quiet acknowledgment

**Process:**
1. Mod team notes inactivity
2. Post in Plaza (no fanfare unless member requested):
   > "Noticing [Name] has been quiet lately. Hoping everything is okay. Return anytime—we'll be here."
3. If member returns: welcome back, no explanation needed

## Milestone Rituals

### Contributor Promotion

**When:** When a Resident becomes Contributor  
**Where:** The Plaza  
**Format:** Recognition

**Process:**
1. Post recognizing the promotion
2. Share what they've contributed
3. Community celebrates

**Sample:**
> **[Name] is now a Contributor!** 🌟
> 
> [Name] has been [describe contributions]. Thank you for enriching our community!

---

### Elder Recognition

**When:** When a Contributor becomes Elder  
**Where:** The Plaza + The Hearth  
**Format:** Dual celebration + tribute

**Process:**
1. Formal announcement in Plaza
2. Tribute thread in Hearth (stories, memories)
3. Elder shares thoughts

---

### Council Installation

**When:** After Council election  
**Where:** The Plaza + Council Chamber  
**Format:** Formal + warm

**Process:**
1. Announcement with candidate statements
2. Incoming Council shares commitment
3. Community welcomes

---

## Crisis Rituals

### Healing Circle

**When:** After community trauma (harassment, conflict, loss)  
**Where:** The Quiet Room (or appropriate space)  
**Format:** Supported, gentle

**Purpose:** Process together  
**Facilitation:** Elder or trusted member

**Structure:**
1. "What happened for you?"
2. "What do you need?"
3. "What can we do together?"

---

### Trust Repair

**When:** After violation requiring restoration  
**Where:** Appropriate (private or public based on severity)  
**Format:** Structured dialogue

**Purpose:** Repair harm, rebuild trust  
**Facilitation:** Council or Mod

**Structure:**
1. Acknowledgment of harm
2. Perspective sharing
3. Agreement on moving forward
4. Follow-up check-in

## Observances

### Foundation Day

**When:** Original founding date  
**Where:** All channels  
**Format:** Anniversary celebration

**Purpose:** Remember why we started  
**Atmosphere:** Nostalgic, grateful, hopeful

---

### Charter Day

**When:** Date of Charter ratification  
**Where:** The Plaza  
**Format:** Reading + reflection

**Purpose:** Re-commit to our principles  
**Activity:** Reading key Charter sections together

---

### Day of Silence

**When:** Once per quarter (optional)  
**Where:** All channels  
**Format:** No posting (observation only)

**Purpose:** Rest, reflect, observe  
**Note:** Not mandatory—just an option for those who want it

## Ritual Hosting

### Who Can Host
- Any Contributor+ can propose a new ritual
- Mod team coordinates weekly/monthly
- Council oversees quarterly

### Hosting Guidelines
1. Set clear start/end times
2. Welcome newcomers
3. Don't force participation
4. End on time
5. Thank participants

## Creating New Rituals

### Proposal
1. Post in Plaza: "Ritual Proposal: [Name]"
2. Describe purpose, format, frequency
3. 7-day discussion
4. Lazy consensus approval

### Guidelines
- Must align with Commons values
- Should be inclusive
- Must not be mandatory
- Should be sustainable

---

## Ritual Evolution

Rituals may be:
- Modified by proposal (same process as creation)
- Archived if no longer serving purpose
- Reactivated if missed
